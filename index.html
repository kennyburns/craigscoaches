Craig's Coaches provides dependable passenger transport services for communities, businesses and visitors across Scotland. From local routes to private hire and event transport, we help people travel safely, comfortably and on time.

Whether you are organising transport for a wedding, a corporate away day, a school group, or a private tour of the Highlands, we bring the same attention to detail to every journey. Pick-up points, timings, passenger numbers and arrivals: all planned properly so you can focus on the occasion.

Why travel with us

  • Local knowledge

    Routes planned by people who know Scotland's roads, venues and timings.

  • Reliable fleet

    Well-maintained coaches and buses kept to a high standard of comfort and presentation.

  • Friendly drivers

    Professional, courteous and attentive to every passenger on board.

  • Flexible transport options

    From small group transfers to large coach hire — sized to suit your occasion.

What we offer

Transport for every occasion

Private coach and bus hire across Scotland, tailored to the occasion and the group.
